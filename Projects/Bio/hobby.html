Progress of the Great Apocalypse

I am an amateur game developer who began experimenting with RPG Maker XP at the age of 15. Even after 20 years, I'm still deeply involved. In fact, this was my gateway into programming. The initial game I developed was titled 'Ego Brain.' Unfortunately, my computer became infected, leading to the loss of three years' worth of work when I had to format my hard drive. However, I persisted and used this setback as a foundation to embark on a much larger project. Thus, I commenced the most significant creative endeavor of my life, birthing "Progress of the Great Apocalypse".
↓ CLICK TO EXPAND ↓
Imagine a universe of boundless complexity, where cultures and belief systems have woven intricate tapestries around long-lost truths. These verities lie entombed beneath layers of holy scriptures, falsified prophecies, and ever-evolving dogmas, meticulously employed by those in power to manipulate their subjects. Within this ancient cycle of worlds dying and being reborn, celestial beings—initially emotionless instruments designed to steer these cosmic processes—slowly awaken to consciousness, seizing control of their own destinies. The narrative unfolds in the aftermath of apocalypse, birthing a freshly crafted world that centres on a child, cradled in his mother's crimson-stained hands, fleeing from relentless royal guards. Enter Zabu, a child thrust into the 'City of Clouds' after the tragic demise of his father. Here, Zabu, traversing a treacherous path, forms alliances with kindred spirits — mostly beggars and orphans — eventually forging a new family, maturing, and selecting his path. Shall he become a temple servant, a law enforcer, or delve into the enigmatic depths of the criminal underworld? His ascent, marked by ambition, sees him scale the pinnacle, achieving one of the seven illustrious titles: destroyer, guardian of faith, blademaster, master of darkness, elementor, the saint, or lord of souls. As fate would have it, he bears witness to the collapse of the magical dome, an implosion that engulfs the entire city in a cataclysmic maelstrom, leaving him as its lone survivor. He wanders amid diverse dimensions, navigating the recesses of his and his companions' minds, healing the scars of trauma, and gradually unearthing ancient truths while grappling with the enduring ache of losing loved ones. Amid this odyssey, alliances are forged, enemies made, love discovered, and a quest emerges — to fill the profound emptiness that gnaws at his heart. This journey, encapsulated within 'Progress of the Great Apocalypse,' invites you to explore the fabric of reality itself. Delve into the enigmas of divine entities, confront deranged demigods, and face the ultimate choice: should this world persist through eternity or succumb to annihilation, birthing a new reality? Embark upon this sweeping odyssey, charting the cosmic tides as you determine the fate of existence itself.
