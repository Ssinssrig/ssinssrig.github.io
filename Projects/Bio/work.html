Physical Therapy

Yes, I'm a licensed physical therapist, and obtaining this license is probably the greatest achievement in my life. It took me a total of seven years. Was it worth it? Well, yeah... it would be if I planned to stay in the US, but that may not be the case. Anyway, it's a great and rewarding career. Paperwork can be repetitive and tedious, fighting with insurances is a nightmare, some patients are really difficult and have ridiculous expectations, and many people just demand a massage, even if it's not a medical necessity.
Furthermore, you would be surprised how clueless some MD's can be.

But honestly, it's not bad at all. Helping people is very rewarding, and most of the patients are very grateful. I take a lot of pleasure and pride in working as a physical therapist, and I would recommend this profession to anyone.
